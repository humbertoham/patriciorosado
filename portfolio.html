    Elevating the Ordinary: The Essence of Sports Photography

    I've learned that to transform a common sports image into an epic photograph, it's essential to convey the perception that the game is worthy of a professional final, regardless of the venue's size, the number of spectators, or the teams' caliber. In the image, you must seek the ideal angle where our athlete appears formidable, showcasing the largest crowd possible, and most challenging of all, capturing it all during an exceptional play. It's about elevating the ordinary, finding beauty and intensity in every moment, and immortalizing the spirit of sportsmanship through the lens

  • Travel

    Frozen Moments: Capturing Time's Relative Nature

    This photo stands out as one of my most challenging endeavors to date. Captured through a long exposure technique, any slight movement, whether from me or my mom (the model), threatened to disrupt the desired outcome. With a shutter speed of 1/8, my aim was to freeze the model while capturing the motion of the passing truck.

    What inspired me to create this image was contemplating the relative nature of time. For someone in a hurry to reach their destination, waiting at a bus station can feel like an eternity. This photo serves as a reminder of how our perception of time can vary depending on our circumstances and state of mind.

    Ana Betancourt: A Journey into Personal Style

    Meet Ana Betancourt, the first model I ever captured through my lens. In the editing process, I infused the images with a personal touch, unknowingly stumbling upon my unique style and crafting a visual narrative that is distinctly mine.

  • Astronomy

    Moonlit Majesty: Capturing the Essence of the Moon

    Photographing the moon can be a daunting task, especially without the proper lens. To capture its beauty, I pushed the limits of my equipment by increasing the ISO to 6,400 and opening the aperture to 6.3—the maximum my gear could afford.

    With the aid of post-processing, I enhanced the lighting to produce a compelling image that conveys the profound emotions the moon evokes within me. Despite the technical challenges, this photograph serves as a testament to the enduring allure and mesmerizing presence of our celestial companion.
