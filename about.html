About Me

Welcome to my world of photography, where every image tells a story and every moment captured is a piece of art.

I'm Patricio Rosado, a passionate photographer with a lifelong love affair with the lens. From a young age, I found solace and inspiration in the world of visual storytelling, and over the years, that passion has blossomed into a career dedicated to freezing moments in time and immortalizing emotions through photography.

My journey as a photographer is one of constant evolution and growth. With a background in fine arts and a deep appreciation for the beauty of the natural world, I bring a unique perspective to every photograph I take. Each click of the shutter is an opportunity to explore the depths of human emotion, to capture the beauty of the world around us, and to tell stories that resonate with the soul.

At Patricio Rosado Photography, my mission is simple: to create images that inspire, uplift, and evoke a sense of wonder. From weddings and portraits to commercial projects and fine art prints, each photograph is a reflection of my passion for the craft and my commitment to excellence.
